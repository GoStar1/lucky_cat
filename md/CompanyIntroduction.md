Company Profile
United Science and Education Press (Malaysia United Science and Education Press) is a branch publishing institution in Malaysia integrated with multiple global publishers, including those from the United States, the United Kingdom, Singapore, South Korea, Japan, and other countries. It publishes a total of 154 journals, with many indexed by EI, PubMed, and Scopus. Headquartered in Johor Bahru, Malaysia, it serves academic enthusiasts worldwide.

Core Philosophy: Professionalism, Innovation, Openness, Win (Professional, Innovative, Open, Win-Win)

Business Scope
1. International Journal Publishing – Operates international journals covering disciplines such as natural sciences, social sciences, engineering, technology, humanities, and arts.
2. Academic Book Publishing – Provides global academic publishing services.
3. Submission and Review Services – Completes academic pre-review within 3-5 working days.
4. FRGS Funding Support – Offers information on the Fundamental Research Grant Scheme (FRGS) in Southeast Asia.

Core Advantages
- International Editorial Board – Comprises thousands of professional editors from various countries and regions.
- Partnerships – Collaborates with over 100 global publishing institutions.
- Strict Standards – Adheres to international publishing standards and norms.
- Rapid Review – Completes pre-review within 3-5 working days after submission.
- Expert Review – Provides review feedback within approximately one month.
- Full Tracking – Offers tracking services for publication progress and indexing status.

Service Process
1. Submission – Submit via the online system or designated email.
2. Pre-Review – Academic pre-review completed within 3-5 working days.
3. Final Review – After passing pre-review and confirming processing fees, forwarded to the journal department for final review.
4. Acceptance Notice – Issued upon passing final review.
5. Tracking Service – Track publication progress and indexing on the official website, with email notifications on manuscript processing updates.

Target Market
- Global academic researchers.
- Scholars focusing on research grants such as FRGS.
